Rules for Using Charger Locker App

This application is specifically designed to make life easier for those using charging stations in this building. Here are some rules to help us use these functions as efficiently as possible.

  1. When you find a free spot, scan the QR code.
  2. Save this website to your phone's home screen. Tap the share button at the bottom of the screen, then select "Add to Home Screen".
  3. Find your spot in the list of available slots (all slots are numbered).
  4. If you need to modify your booking, on the page with your current reservation info, click "Edit Booking".
  5. When you revisit the website, you'll see information about your active reservation.
  6. If you unplug your car before the reservation ends, you can stop the booking. Then other users will see that the spot is free. If you don't do this, the reservation will automatically expire at the designated time.
  7. If all spots are occupied, the initial page will show you the three spots that will become free the earliest.
