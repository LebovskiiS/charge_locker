Charging Station App Rules

This app is specifically designed to make life easier for everyone who uses the charging stations in this building. Here are a few rules to help us maximize these functions.

  1. As soon as you find a free space, scan the QR code.
  2. Find your spot in the list of available slots (all slots are numbered).
  3. Choose the time for which you would like to book your spot. Please note that if you leave your car overnight to charge, your reservation will be cancelled automatically at 10AM.
  4. When you re-enter the site, your reservation information will be visible.
  5. If you remove your car from the charger before your time has elapsed, you can scan the QR code again and stop the reservation. Then all other users will know that the spot is free.
